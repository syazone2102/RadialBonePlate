What is Micromotion? It is the microscopic movement between bone fragments.
  • 0.2mm - 1.0mm: Stimulates stem cells to form a biological callus (Faster Healing).
  • > 2.0mm: Fragments shift too much, tearing delicate blood vessels. High risk of Non-Union.
