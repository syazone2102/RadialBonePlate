What is In Vivo Force?
This is the actual physical load inside the living body. To stabilize the wrist, muscles like the brachioradialis contract forcefully. This acts as a lever, generating internal compressive and bending forces that are often 3 to 5 times greater than standard gravity. The load multiplier mathematically scales your data to simulate this.

What is Micromotion? It is the microscopic movement between bone fragments.
  • 0.2mm - 1.0mm: Stimulates stem cells to form a biological callus (Faster Healing).
  • > 2.0mm: Fragments shift too much, tearing delicate blood vessels. High risk of Non-Union.
